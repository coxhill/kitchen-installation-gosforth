Installation Timeline

Kitchen Installation Timeline for Gosforth Properties

A realistic, professionally managed installation timeline for a standard Gosforth kitchen project — from strip-out to sign-off.

1Days 1–2: Strip-Out and Preparation

Full removal of existing kitchen, waste disposal, wall and floor preparation. First-fix electrical circuits, socket repositioning, and waste pipe routing completed. Base unit level lines marked and confirmed.

2Days 2–4: Cabinet Installation

Base units positioned on adjustable legs, levelled to within 1mm, and secured. Wall units hung and adjusted. Tall units installed. Cornices, plinths, and fillers fitted. All unit alignments checked and confirmed before proceeding.

3Days 4–5: Worktops and Sink

  • Worktop sections delivered and positioned
  • Joins cut, bonded, and sealed
  • Sink and tap fitted and plumbed
  • All plumbing tested under load before proceeding

4Days 5–6: Appliances and Second Fix

All integrated appliances fitted and connected. Second-fix electrical — sockets, switches, and under-cabinet lighting commissioned. Extractor and cooker hood connected and function-tested.

5Day 6–7: Splashback and Finishing

  • Splashback or upstands fitted and sealed
  • All doors and drawers hung and adjusted
  • Handles fitted to all doors and drawers
  • Plinths and end panel finishing completed
  • Full clean-out of all units and surfaces

6Day 7–8: Snagging and Sign-Off

Systematic review of every adjustment, seal, alignment, and function. All items documented and resolved before written sign-off and workmanship guarantee issued.

Gosforth-specific note: Many Gosforth properties are Victorian or Edwardian with uneven floors and walls that require additional levelling work. We build contingency time into every Gosforth project timeline for the structural preparation that these properties typically require.
